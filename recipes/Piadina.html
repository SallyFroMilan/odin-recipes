Piadina farcita

An easy and delicious Italian recipe, for all tastes and for all seasons

INGREDIENTS

  • PIADINA ROMAGNOLA
  • RUCOLA
  • SCUAQUERONE OR STRACCHINO CHEESE
  • FINELY SLICED PROSCIUTTO CRUDO

STEPS

  1. Clean the rocket by removing the tough parts of the stalks, wash and dry it by dabbing it with a tea towel or salad spinner
  2. Season the piadina as it is cooked according to the following recipe:
    1. Spread the entire piada with a layer of squacquerone cheese.
    2. Place the prosciutto and rocket on half of the piada.
    3. Close and serve immediately.
