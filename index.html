Italian cuisine boasts the greatest eno-gastronomic vastness: with more than 5800 products, it is the Country with the largest number of agri-food products with denomination of origin and geographical indication recognised by the EU.

Part of Mediterranean diet (considered the healthiest diet in the World), Italian cuisine between sustainability and biocultural diversity is also a candidate for UNESCO intangible heritage status.
